ABOUT

Established in 2000, ERKE is one of the leading sports brands in China with comprehensive capabilities of R&D,
manufacture,channel development and marketing with nearly 30,000 employees of ERKE sportswear series to provide
the mass market in China with professional sporting products including footwear, apparel and accessories.
With over 7000 mono-brand stores spreading across China and more than 1000 sales points overseas,
ERKE has product available in Europe, southeast Asia, the Middle East, North and South America, Africa and other countries
and significant opportunities for continued expansion worldwide.The company has exclusive trademark rights in more than
100 countries, with the brand value exceeding 3 billion USD in 2017.ERKE aims to be one of the leading sports brands in the world.
